Section 1 — Accessing & Logging In

Opening the portal and signing into your client account

Opening the App

  1. Open your web browser and navigate to the Property Management App URL.

  2. On the landing page, click the Client Portal card — it flips to reveal the login form.

  3. Enter your Username and Password. Use the eye icon (👁) to reveal the password if needed.

  4. Click "Sign In" (or press Enter). You will land on your Client Dashboard.

📝

Section 2 — First-Time Setup

Creating your login credentials for the first time

Before you begin: You need your Client Code (e.g. CL001) and the telephone number registered with your agency.

  1. On the Client login card, click "First Time Setup →".

  2. Fill in all fields:

FieldWhat to EnterRequired
Client CodeYour client reference code (e.g. CL001) from your agencyYes
TelephoneTelephone number registered with your agencyYes
New UsernameChoose a username for logging inYes
New PasswordChoose a secure password (minimum 6 characters)Yes
Confirm PasswordRe-enter your chosen passwordYes

  1. Click "Set Credentials". A success message confirms your credentials are saved.

  2. Click "← Back to Login" and sign in with your new details.

🔓

Section 3 — Resetting a Forgotten Password

Recovering your account access

  1. On the Client login card, click "Forgot Password? →".

  2. Enter your Username and click "Send Code". Your email app opens with a reset code.

  3. Send the email. The form advances to Phase 2 automatically.

  4. Enter the 6-digit reset code, your New Password, and Confirm Password.

  5. Click "Reset Password". You will be returned to the login screen.

Important: The reset code expires in 15 minutes.

Section 5 — Managing Properties

Add, edit, and manage your property portfolio

Viewing Properties

  1. Click "My Properties" in the sidebar.

  2. A table lists all your properties with: Code, Name, Location, Type, Manager, Currency, and Active status.

Adding a New Property

  1. Click "Add Property" (top right).

  2. Fill in the form:

FieldDescriptionRequired
Property NameFull name of the property (e.g. Green View Apartments)Yes
TypeApartment, Villa, Loft, Condo, House, Suite, or CommercialYes
Country / CityWhere the property is locatedYes
Address / LocationStreet address or detailed locationOptional
ManagerName of the property managerOptional
Manager PhoneManager's contact numberOptional
CurrencyUSD, EUR, GBP, CAD, KES, TZS, or UGXYes
ActiveYes (property is currently operational) or NoYes
NotesAny additional information about the propertyOptional
Property PictureUpload a photo of the property (JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP)Optional

  1. Click "Save Property". The property is added and a unique property code is generated automatically.

Editing a Property

  1. In the Properties table, click the pencil icon (✏) on the property row.

  2. Update any fields (Name, Type, City, Country, Location, Manager, Currency, Active, Notes).

  3. Click "Save Changes".

Deleting a Property

  1. Click the trash icon (🗑) on the property row.

  2. Confirm the deletion in the pop-up dialog.

Note: A property cannot be deleted if it has active tenants. First deactivate or move all tenants, then delete the property.

Section 6 — Managing Property Units

Add, edit, search, and bulk-upload units

Viewing Units

  1. Click "Property Units" in the sidebar.

  2. Units are grouped by property. Click a property header to expand or collapse its units.

  3. Use the search bar to filter by property name or unit number.

  4. Use the status buttons (All / Occupied / Vacant) to filter by unit status.

Adding a Single Unit

  1. Click "Add Unit" (top right).

  2. Select the Property from the dropdown, then fill in:

FieldDescriptionRequired
Unit Numbere.g. 101, A2, Ground Floor (auto-uppercased)Yes
Rent AmountMonthly rent in the property's currencyYes
Deposit AmountSecurity deposit amountYes
BedroomsNumber of bedrooms (default: 1)Yes
BathroomsNumber of bathrooms (default: 1)Yes
FloorFloor number (default: 1)Yes
StatusVacant (default) or OccupiedYes
NotesAdditional details about the unitOptional

  1. Click "Save Unit". A unique unit code is auto-generated.

Bulk Uploading Units (Excel / CSV)

  1. Click "Units Upload" (top right).

  2. Click "Unit Upload Template" to download the Excel template.

  3. Open the template and fill in one unit per row. Required columns: PropertyCode, UnitNumber, RentAmount, DepositAmount, Bedrooms, Bathrooms, Floor, UnitStatus.

  4. Save the file, then click the file input in the upload dialog and select your saved file.

  5. A preview table appears showing each row and its validation status (OK or error details).

  6. Only valid rows will be imported. Click "Import X Unit(s)" to complete the import.

Common errors: PropertyCode not found (must match exactly), Unit already exists for that property, missing numeric values.

Editing a Unit

Click the ✏ pencil icon on the unit row → update fields → click "Save".

Deleting a Unit

Click the 🗑 trash icon. Units cannot be deleted if they are Occupied or have associated tenants/transactions.

Section 7 — Managing Tenants

Add, edit, upload, and manage your tenants

Viewing Tenants

  1. Click "Tenants" in the sidebar. Tenants are grouped by property.

  2. Use the search bar to find by name, unit number, or tenant number.

  3. Click a property header to expand or collapse that property's tenant list.

Adding a New Tenant

  1. Click "Add Tenant" (top right).

  2. Fill in all required fields:

FieldDescriptionRequired
First Name / Last NameTenant's full nameYes
ID NumberNational ID or passport numberYes
TelephoneTenant's mobile/phone numberYes
EmailTenant's email addressYes
AddressTenant's home/mailing addressYes
PropertySelect from your propertiesYes
UnitSelect from units in the chosen propertyYes
Rent AmountAgreed monthly/periodic rentYes
FrequencyMonthly, Quarterly, SemiAnnually, or AnnuallyYes
Deposit AmountSecurity deposit collectedYes
Move-in DateDate the tenant moved inYes
PhotographTenant photo (JPG, JPEG, PNG)Yes
ID CopyScan/photo of ID document (PDF, JPG, PNG)Yes
Rent AgreementSigned lease/agreement document (PDF, JPG, DOC, DOCX)Yes

  1. Click "Save Tenant". A unique tenant number is auto-generated (e.g. TN-001).

Bulk Uploading Tenants

  1. Click "Tenants Upload" → "Tenants Upload Template" to download the template.

  2. Fill in one tenant per row. See the Field Notes sheet in the template for allowed values.

  3. Upload the file, review the preview table, then click "Import X Tenant(s)".

  4. After import, attach documents (photo, ID, lease) for each tenant via Edit Tenant.

Editing a Tenant

  1. Click the ✏ pencil icon on the tenant row.

  2. Update contact info, rent amount, move-out date, or active status as needed.

  3. To replace a document (photograph, ID copy, or rent agreement), click the relevant file field and select the new file. Leave blank to keep the existing document.

  4. Click "Save".

Deleting a Tenant

Click the 🗑 trash icon. A tenant cannot be deleted if they are Active or have any transactions on record. First set Active = No and ensure there are no transactions.

Section 8 — Managing Transactions

Post, view, edit, and bulk-post tenant transactions

Viewing Transactions

  1. Click "Transactions" in the sidebar.

  2. All transactions are listed with: Date, Tenant, Property, Description, Amount, Reference, and Status.

  3. The total portfolio balance is shown in the header.

Posting a Single Transaction

  1. Click "Post Transaction" (top right).

  2. Select the Property — the tenant list will automatically show only active tenants in that property.

  3. Select the Tenant from the right dropdown.

  4. Enter the Amount (positive for credit/payment received; negative for a debit/charge).

  5. Enter a Description (e.g. "Rent Payment — May 2026").

  6. Add an optional Note and upload a supporting Document (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC, DOCX — required).

  7. Click "Post". A transaction reference number is generated automatically.

Bulk Posting Transactions

Use this to post the same charge (e.g. monthly rent) to multiple tenants at once.

  1. Click "Bulk Posting" (top right).

  2. Select a Date and Property. Active tenants will be listed with checkboxes.

  3. Choose Charge Type: "Monthly Rent" (uses each tenant's set rent) or "Custom Charge" (enter a fixed amount).

  4. Enter a Description and optional Note.

  5. Check/uncheck tenants to include. Use "Select All" or "None" to manage the selection quickly.

  6. Click "Bulk Post". One transaction is created per selected tenant.

Editing a Transaction

Click the ✏ pencil icon → update Date, Amount, Description, Note, or replace the document → click "Save Changes".

Deleting a Transaction

Click the 🗑 trash icon. Only Pending transactions can be deleted. Verified transactions cannot be deleted.

Section 9 — Print Reports

Generate, print, and export property and tenant reports

Accessing Reports: Click "Print Reports" in the sidebar. Four report tabs are available at the top.

Report 1: Property Transactions

  1. Click the "Property Transactions" tab.

  2. Select a Property, enter a Date From and Date To.

  3. Click "Generate". A full transaction report shows with Debit, Credit, and running Balance columns plus a Closing Balance.

  4. Click "Print", "Export Excel", or "Export CSV" as needed.

Report 2: Tenant Statements

  1. Click the "Tenant Statements" tab.

  2. Optionally select a Property to filter the tenant list to that property only.

  3. A list of tenants appears with checkboxes — all are selected by default.

  4. Tick/untick tenants as needed. Use "Select All" or "None" to manage quickly.

  5. Click "Generate". One statement per selected tenant is produced, each with a full transaction history, running balance, and closing balance.

  6. Click "Print" to print all statements (each on its own page), or export to Excel/CSV.

Report 3: List of Tenants

  1. Click the "List of Tenants" tab.

  2. Optionally filter by Property and/or Status (All, Active, Inactive).

  3. Click "Generate". A table lists Tenant#, Name, ID, Property, Unit, Rent, Move-in, Move-out, and Status.

  4. Print or export as needed.

Report 4: Units List

  1. Click the "Units List" tab.

  2. Filter by Property and/or Status (All, Occupied, Vacant).

  3. Click "Generate". A table lists Unit Code, Unit#, Property, Rent, Deposit, Status, and Active.

  4. Print or export as needed.

Section 10 — Profile, Settings & Sign Out

Manage your account details and preferences

Updating Your Profile

  1. Click "My Profile" in the sidebar.

  2. Update your Client Name, Email, Telephone, or Address.

  3. To change your password, enter the New Password and Confirm New Password in the Change Password section.

  4. Click "Save Changes".

Dark / Light Mode

  1. Click the 🌙 moon icon at the top right to switch to dark mode.

  2. Click the ☀️ sun icon to switch back to light mode. Your preference is saved automatically.

Signing Out

  1. Click "Sign Out" at the bottom of the sidebar to log out securely.

Security reminder: Always sign out when using a shared computer. Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
