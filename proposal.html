Platform Features

Four Dedicated Portals
Master Admin Portal
Full system visibility — manage all agencies, clients, properties, units, tenants and transactions from a single control centre. Bulk-import data via Excel and generate cross-portfolio reports.
Agency Portal
Agencies manage their assigned clients and portfolios, post transactions on behalf of tenants, generate statements and handle day-to-day property operations under master oversight.
Client (Landlord) Portal
Property owners log in to view their full portfolio — occupied and vacant units, tenant details, transaction history and financial reports — all filtered to their own properties.
Tenant Portal
Tenants access their personal dashboard, view lease details, post rent payments, browse full transaction history and print account statements — securely and independently.
Core Management Capabilities
Property Management Add, edit and track properties with full client linkage and status monitoring.
Unit Management Track every unit's rent, deposit, occupancy status and tenant assignment.
Tenant Management Full tenant records including ID, contact info, lease dates and rental history.
Transaction Ledger Post credits and debits with running balance, references and verification status.
Bulk Excel Import Upload units and tenants in bulk using downloadable Excel templates with built-in validation.
Live Dashboard At-a-glance KPIs — portfolio size, occupancy rate, revenue and recent activity.

No Installation Required

Each user sees only the data relevant to their role. Master, agency, client and tenant access are strictly separated with secure login and password reset.

3

Multi-Agency Support

One master account can oversee multiple agencies and their respective client portfolios — perfect for growing property management firms.

4

Complete Financial Audit Trail

Every transaction is recorded with date, reference, amount and verification status — giving you a clear, tamper-evident financial ledger.

5

Instant Reports & Exports

Generate client, property and tenant reports in seconds. Print directly or export to Excel and CSV for your own analysis and accounting.

6

Scalable Bulk Data Entry

No need to add units or tenants one by one. Download our Excel templates, fill in your data and upload hundreds of records in minutes with built-in validation.

A polished, modern interface with light and dark theme support — comfortable for use in any environment, any time of day.
