Section 1 — Accessing the App

Open the Property Management App in your web browser

Opening the Login Page

  1. Open your preferred web browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).

  2. Type the web address provided by your property manager into the address bar and press Enter.

  3. You will see the Property Management landing page with four portal cards: Tenant, Client, Agency, and Master Admin.

  4. Click the Tenant Portal card — it will flip to reveal the login form.

Tip: Bookmark the app URL in your browser so you can return to it quickly each time.
📝

Section 2 — First-Time Setup

Creating your username and password for the first time

Before you begin: You will need your Property Code and Tenant Code, and the telephone number registered with your property manager. Contact your manager if you do not have these.

Creating Your Login Credentials

  1. On the Tenant login card, click "First Time Setup →" below the Sign In button.

  2. Fill in all the required fields:

FieldWhat to EnterRequired
Property CodeThe code for your property (e.g. PR001) — provided by your managerYes
Tenant CodeYour tenant reference code (e.g. TN-2026-001) — provided by your managerYes
TelephoneThe phone number registered with your property managerYes
New UsernameChoose a username you will use to log in (e.g. john.doe)Yes
New PasswordChoose a secure password — minimum 6 charactersYes
Confirm PasswordRe-type your chosen password exactlyYes

  1. Click "Set Credentials". A green success message will appear confirming your credentials have been saved.

  2. Click "← Back to Login" and proceed to sign in with your new username and password.

Important: Remember your username and password. Store them in a safe place. If you forget, use the "Forgot Password?" option on the login card.
🚪

Section 3 — Logging In

Signing into your tenant account

Steps to Sign In

  1. On the Tenant Portal card, enter your Property Code (e.g. PR001).

  2. Enter your Username.

  3. Enter your Password. Click the eye icon (👁) on the right of the password field to reveal your password if needed.

  4. Click "Sign In". You will be taken directly to your Tenant Dashboard.

Note: You can also press the Enter key instead of clicking Sign In.
Login failed? Check that your Property Code, Username, and Password are all correct. Ensure Caps Lock is off. If you still cannot log in, use "Forgot Password?" or contact your property manager.
🔓

Section 4 — Resetting a Forgotten Password

Recovering access when you have forgotten your password

Step 1 — Request a Reset Code

  1. On the Tenant login card, click "Forgot Password? →".

  2. Enter your Property Code and Username.

  3. Click "Send Code". Your email application will open with a pre-filled message containing a 6-digit reset code.

  4. Send the email. A confirmation message will appear — the form will automatically advance to Step 2 after a moment.

Step 2 — Set Your New Password

  1. Enter the 6-digit code from the email you received.

  2. Enter your New Password (minimum 6 characters) and Confirm Password.

  3. Click "Reset Password". A success message will appear and you will be returned to the login screen.

  4. Log in with your new password.

Important: The reset code expires in 15 minutes. If it expires, click "← Back" and request a new code.

Section 5 — Dashboard Overview

Understanding your account at a glance

After logging in, you land on your Dashboard. This is the home screen of your tenant portal.

What You Can See on the Dashboard

🏢 Property & Unit Details

  • Your property name and code
  • Your unit number, bedrooms, bathrooms, and floor
  • Your move-in date

💰 Account Balance

  • Your current account balance shown in large text
  • Green balance = credit (you have paid more than owed)
  • Red balance = amount outstanding

📋 Rent Information

  • Your monthly rent amount
  • Deposit amount paid
  • Payment frequency

🗓 Recent Transactions

  • Last 5 transactions on your account
  • Date, description, amount, and status
  • Click "View All" to see all transactions

Navigating the Portal

  1. Use the sidebar (left panel) to move between sections. On mobile, tap the ☰ menu icon at the top left to open the sidebar.

  2. The current section is highlighted in green in the sidebar.

  3. Click "Sign Out" in the sidebar to log out when you are done.

Section 6 — Updating Your Profile

Keeping your contact details and password up to date

Updating Your Personal Details

  1. Click "My Profile" in the sidebar.

  2. Your profile card shows your name, tenant number, property, unit, and account status.

  3. Update any of the following fields as needed:

FieldDescription
First NameYour first name
Last NameYour last name
TelephoneYour current phone number
EmailYour email address
AddressYour current home/mailing address

  1. Click "Save Changes" to save your updated information. A green success toast will confirm.

Changing Your Password

  1. Scroll down to the "Change Password" section on the My Profile page.

  2. Enter your New Password (minimum 6 characters).

  3. Re-enter it in the Confirm New Password field.

  4. Click "Save Changes". Your password will be updated immediately.

Tip: You only need to fill in the password fields if you want to change your password. Leave them blank to update only your contact details.

Section 7 — Posting a Transaction

Recording a rent payment or other transaction on your account

What is a transaction? A transaction is any payment or charge on your account — such as rent paid, a deposit, or a fee. Every transaction must include a supporting document (e.g. a bank receipt or payment confirmation).

How to Post a Transaction

  1. Click "Post Transaction" in the sidebar.

  2. Fill in the form:

FieldWhat to EnterRequired
DateThe date the payment was made (defaults to today)Yes
CurrencyAuto-filled based on your property — cannot be changed—
DescriptionBrief description, e.g. "Rent Payment — May 2026"Yes
AmountThe payment amount. Enter a positive number for payments you make. Use a negative number only for a debit/charge.Yes
NoteAny additional information (e.g. bank reference number)Optional
DocumentUpload your payment receipt or proof (PDF, JPG, PNG, DOC, DOCX)Yes

  1. Click the "Document" field and select your payment proof file from your device.

  2. Click "Submit Transaction". A success message will confirm the transaction was recorded.

  3. Your transaction will appear with status Pending until verified by your property manager.

Tip: After paying your rent, take a clear photo or screenshot of your bank/M-PESA confirmation and upload it as your document. This speeds up verification.
Note: Transactions show as Pending until your property manager or agency reviews and verifies them. You will see them change to Verified once approved.
📋

Section 8 — Viewing Your Transactions

Reviewing all payments and charges on your account

Viewing the Transactions List

  1. Click "My Transactions" in the sidebar.

  2. Your full transaction history is displayed in a table showing:

ColumnWhat It Shows
DateThe date the transaction was posted
DescriptionWhat the transaction is for
AmountThe transaction amount (green = credit, red = debit)
StatusVerified or Pending

  1. Your current account balance is shown at the top of the page.

  2. To view the details of a transaction, click on the amount — a detail panel will open showing the reference number, note, and any attached document.

Tip: If you see a transaction you do not recognise, contact your property manager with the transaction reference number.
🖨️

Section 9 — Printing Your Account Statement

Generating a printable record of all your transactions

Generating and Printing a Statement

  1. Click "Print Statement" in the sidebar.

  2. Your full account statement is displayed automatically. It includes:

📄 Statement Header

  • Your full name and tenant number
  • Property name and unit number
  • Monthly rent amount
  • Statement date (today)

📊 Transaction Table

  • Date, Description, Reference
  • Debit column (amounts charged to you)
  • Credit column (payments you made)
  • Running balance after each transaction
  • Closing balance at the bottom

  1. Review the statement on screen.

  2. When ready, click the "Print" button. Your browser's print dialog will open.

  3. Select your printer or choose "Save as PDF" to save a digital copy.

  4. Click Print to complete.

Tip: To save paper, choose "Save as PDF" and email the PDF statement to your property manager or keep it for your records.
⚙️

Section 10 — General Settings & Sign Out

Theme settings and logging out of your account

Switching Between Dark and Light Mode

  1. Click the moon icon (🌙) at the top-right of any page to switch to dark mode.

  2. Click the sun icon (☀️) to switch back to light mode.

  3. Your preference is saved automatically and remembered the next time you visit.

Signing Out

  1. Click "Sign Out" at the bottom of the sidebar (or hamburger menu on mobile).

  2. You will be taken back to the main login page immediately.

Security tip: Always sign out when using a shared or public computer to protect your account.

Section 11 — Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common tenant questions

Q: I cannot log in — what should I do?

Ensure you are entering the correct Property Code, Username, and Password. Check that Caps Lock is off. If you are still unable to log in, use "Forgot Password?" on the login card or contact your property manager.

Q: My transaction shows "Pending" — is that normal?

Yes. All transactions you post start as Pending and must be reviewed and verified by your property manager or agency. This typically takes 1–2 business days.

Q: Can I edit or delete a transaction I posted?

No. Once posted, tenants cannot edit or delete transactions. If you made an error, contact your property manager with the transaction reference number to have it corrected.

Q: I forgot to attach a document — what do I do?

A document is required to post a transaction. If you cannot upload one immediately, gather your payment receipt and post the transaction again. Contact your manager to explain the situation.

Q: My account balance looks wrong — who do I contact?

Print your account statement (Section 9) and compare it against your own records. Contact your property manager with your tenant number and any discrepancies you find.

Q: Can I change my username?

Usernames cannot be changed after initial setup. If you need a new username, contact your property manager.

Need further help? Contact your property manager or agency for any issues not covered in this manual. Have your Tenant Number ready when you call or email.
