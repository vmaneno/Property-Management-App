Section 1 — Accessing & Logging In

Opening the portal and signing into your agency account

Agency Portal Data Hierarchy

Understanding the structure your agency manages:

🏙️ Agency (your organisation)
→ 👔 Clients (property owners you manage)
→ 🏢 Properties (buildings/estates per client)
→ 🚪 Units (individual rentable spaces)
→ 👤 Tenants (occupants of units)
→ 💳 Transactions (payments & charges)

Logging In

  1. Navigate to the Property Management App URL in your browser.

  2. Click the Agency Portal card on the landing page.

  3. Enter your Username and Password. Use the eye icon (👁) to reveal the password if needed.

  4. Click "Sign In" (or press Enter).

📝

Section 2 — First-Time Setup

Creating your agency login credentials

Before you begin: You need your Agency Code (e.g. AG001) and the telephone number registered with the Master Administrator.

  1. On the Agency login card, click "First Time Setup →".

  2. Fill in all fields:

FieldWhat to EnterRequired
Agency CodeYour agency code provided by the Master Admin (e.g. AG001)Yes
TelephoneTelephone number registered in the systemYes
New UsernameChoose a username for your agency loginYes
New PasswordChoose a secure password (minimum 6 characters)Yes
Confirm PasswordRe-enter your chosen passwordYes

  1. Click "Set Credentials". A success message confirms registration.

  2. Click "← Back to Login" and sign in.

🔓

Section 3 — Resetting a Forgotten Password

Recovering access to your agency account

  1. On the Agency login card, click "Forgot Password? →".

  2. Enter your Username and click "Send Code". Your email app opens with a reset code.

  3. Send the email. The system advances to Phase 2 automatically.

  4. Enter the 6-digit reset code, your New Password, and Confirm Password.

  5. Click "Reset Password". Sign in with your new password.

Important: Reset codes expire in 15 minutes. Request a new code if needed.

Section 5 — Managing Clients

Add and manage the property owners under your agency

Viewing Clients

  1. Click "Clients" in the sidebar.

  2. The table shows: Code, Name, Email, Telephone, City/Country, Currency, Active status, and Actions.

Adding a New Client

  1. Click "Add Client" (top right).

  2. Fill in the form:

FieldDescriptionRequired
Client NameFull name or company name of the property ownerYes
Contact PersonPrimary contact person (if company)Yes
TelephoneClient's primary phone numberYes
EmailClient's email addressYes
AddressClient's mailing addressOptional
City / CountryLocation of clientOptional
CurrencyDefault currency for this client (USD, EUR, GBP, CAD, KES, TZS, UGX)Optional
ActiveYes (client is currently active) or NoYes

  1. Click "Save Client". A unique client code is auto-generated.

Editing a Client

  1. Click the ✏ pencil icon on the client row.

  2. Update the required fields and click "Save Changes".

Deleting a Client

  1. Click the 🗑 trash icon on the client row.

  2. Confirm deletion in the dialog.

Note: A client cannot be deleted if they are Active or have linked properties, tenants, or transactions. Deactivate the client and remove associated records first.

Section 7 — Managing Units

Add, edit, and bulk upload property units

Viewing Units

  1. Click "Units" in the sidebar. Units are grouped by property.

  2. Use the search bar and status filter (All, Occupied, Vacant) to narrow results.

  3. Click a property header to expand or collapse its unit list.

Adding a Single Unit

  1. Click "Add Unit" (top right).

  2. Select the Property, then fill in: Unit Number, Rent Amount, Deposit Amount, Bedrooms, Bathrooms, Floor, Status (Vacant is default), and Notes.

  3. Click "Save Unit".

Bulk Uploading Units (Excel / CSV)

  1. Click "Units Upload" → "Unit Upload Template" to download the Excel template.

  2. Fill in one unit per row. Required columns: PropertyCode, UnitNumber, RentAmount, DepositAmount, Bedrooms, Bathrooms, Floor, UnitStatus.

  3. Upload the completed file. A preview table shows validation results row by row.

  4. Click "Import X Unit(s)" to import all valid rows.

Note: Only rows that pass all validation checks will be imported. Fix errors in your file and re-upload if needed.

Editing a Unit

Click the ✏ pencil icon on the unit row → update the fields → click "Save".

Deleting a Unit

Click the 🗑 trash icon. Units cannot be deleted if Occupied or associated with active tenants or transactions.

Section 9 — Managing Transactions

Post, verify, edit, and bulk-process transactions

Posting a Single Transaction

  1. Click "Transactions" in the sidebar → "Post Transaction" (top right).

  2. Select Client. The Property dropdown will cascade to show only that client's properties.

  3. Select the Property. The Tenant dropdown cascades to only active tenants on that property.

  4. Enter: Date, Amount (positive = credit, negative = debit), Description.

  5. Add an optional Note and upload a supporting Document (required).

  6. Click "Post". A transaction reference is generated automatically.

Bulk Posting Transactions

  1. Click "Bulk Posting" (top right).

  2. Select Client → Property (cascading dropdowns). Active tenants appear as checkboxes.

  3. Choose Charge Type: Monthly Rent (uses each tenant's rent) or Custom Charge (fixed amount).

  4. Enter a Description and optional Note.

  5. Check the tenants to include (all are checked by default). Use "Select All" / "None" for quick selection.

  6. Click "Bulk Post". One transaction is posted per selected tenant.

Editing a Transaction

Click ✏ on the transaction row → update Date, Amount, Description, Note, or replace the document → click "Save Changes".

Deleting a Transaction

Click 🗑. Only Pending transactions can be deleted. Verified transactions are locked.

Section 10 — Print Reports

Generate and export reports across your agency portfolio

Accessing Reports: Click "Print Reports" in the sidebar. Select a tab from the four available report types.

Report 1: Property Transactions

  1. Select a Property, enter Date From and Date To, then click "Generate".

  2. The report shows all transactions in the date range with Debit, Credit, and running Balance columns.

  3. Click Print, Export Excel, or Export CSV.

Report 2: Tenant Statements

  1. Optionally choose a Property to filter the tenant list.

  2. Select tenants using the checkboxes (all selected by default).

  3. Click "Generate". One full statement per selected tenant is produced.

  4. Print or export as needed.

Report 3: List of Tenants

Filter by Property and/or Status → click "Generate" → print or export the full tenant register.

Report 4: Units List

Filter by Property and/or Status (All / Occupied / Vacant) → click "Generate" → print or export.

Section 11 — Profile, Settings & Sign Out

Update agency details and manage your session

Updating Your Profile

  1. Click "My Profile" in the sidebar.

  2. Update your Agency Name, Email, Telephone, or Address.

  3. To change your password, complete the Change Password section (New Password + Confirm New Password).

  4. Click "Save Changes".

Dark / Light Mode

Click the 🌙 moon or ☀️ sun icon at the top right to switch themes. Your preference is saved automatically.

Signing Out

Click "Sign Out" at the bottom of the sidebar.

Security reminder: Always sign out after each session, especially on shared devices. Do not share your login credentials.
Need help? Contact your Master Administrator with your Agency Code for any account issues or access problems.
